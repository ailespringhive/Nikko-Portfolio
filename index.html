Collector & Technician Apps

Technician Mobile and Collector Mobile are connected applications used for managing and monitoring technician tasks, customer services, and payment activities efficiently.

The Technician Mobile application allows technicians to view assigned tickets, track work progress, manage customer information, update profiles, and handle service-related activities through a mobile platform.

Meanwhile, the Collector Mobile application enables collectors to manage customer payment information, track payments, monitor unpaid and overdue accounts, manage customer records, and generate summaries or reports. It also supports receipt generation and can print receipts using a portable thermal printer for faster and more convenient transaction processing.
